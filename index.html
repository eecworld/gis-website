EEC is focused on your success.

From geographic information system (GIS) design to asset management implementation, EEC’s technology professionals assist in all phases of system implementation, from planning to rollout to maintenance.

EEC matches the appropriate technology to clients’ business needs and deploys these solutions to ensure that they remain effective well into the future. Business partnerships with companies like Esri, Cityworks, and Amazon Web Services give EEC access to the latest in GIS and information technology software, infrastructure, and best practices. Whether you have a small- or large-scale project or need enterprise implementation, EEC’s Technology Services team will help you be successful.
